Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Speechy ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Speechy application for macOS and iOS, the Speechy website, and our licensing services (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Speechy is designed as a privacy-first application where speech processing occurs entirely on your device. We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate our licensing system.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect, as this is central to our privacy commitment:

  • Audio recordings: We never record, transmit, access, store, or process your voice or audio data on our servers. All speech recognition occurs locally on your device.
  • Transcription content: The text output of your transcriptions is stored only on your device. We have no access to it and it is never sent to our servers.
  • Usage analytics or telemetry: We do not track how you use the Application, which features you use, how often you use them, or for how long.
  • Location data: We do not access or collect your geographic location.
  • Contacts, calendar, photos, or other personal data: We do not access any personal data on your device beyond microphone access for speech recognition.
  • IP addresses: We do not log IP addresses in our application infrastructure.
  • Cookies or tracking pixels: Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics services.
  • Advertising identifiers: We do not collect or use advertising identifiers. We have no advertising of any kind.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • License delivery: To send you your license key via email verification.
  • License verification: To validate that your license is active and has not expired.
  • Device management: To enforce device activation limits as specified by your license type.
  • Fraud prevention: To prevent abuse of trial licenses and the licensing system.
  • Customer support: To assist you with license-related issues when you contact us.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, profiling, behavioral analysis, or any purpose other than those listed above.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The Application stores the following data locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and we have no access to it:

  • Transcription history: Your recent transcriptions are stored locally for your convenience. On macOS, this is stored in application preferences (limited to the 50 most recent entries). On iOS, this is stored in the application's local document directory.
  • Application settings: Your hotkey configurations, language preferences, model selection, and other settings are stored locally.
  • AI models: Downloaded speech recognition models are stored on your device's local storage.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the Application or by clearing the Application's data through your operating system's settings.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 AI Model Downloads

The Application downloads speech recognition model files from HuggingFace (huggingface.co), a third-party model hosting platform. During this download:

  • Only standard HTTP request headers are transmitted (no personal data).
  • No account with HuggingFace is required.
  • The download is a one-time event per model; once downloaded, the model operates fully offline.
  • HuggingFace's own privacy policy governs their handling of any connection data.

5.2 Payment Processors

If you purchase a paid license, payment is processed by third-party payment processors. We do not store your payment card details, banking information, or other financial data on our servers. Payment processors handle your financial data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable regulations (such as PCI DSS).

5.3 No Other Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to any other third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media plugins, or content delivery networks that track users.

6. Data Retention

After the applicable retention period, data is permanently deleted from our systems. You may request earlier deletion as described in Section 8.

7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit: All communications between the Application and our licensing servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure license keys: License keys are generated using cryptographically secure random number generation (192-bit entropy).
  • Prepared statements: All database queries use parameterized prepared statements to prevent SQL injection attacks.
  • Timing-safe authentication: Administrative API authentication uses constant-time comparison to prevent timing attacks.
  • Input validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized before processing.
  • Access control: Administrative access to license data is restricted by API key authentication.

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right of Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. Contact us and we will provide this information within 30 days.

8.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold about you.

8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our systems. Upon such request, we will delete your email address, license records, and associated activation data. Note that this will permanently invalidate any active licenses associated with your account.

8.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (JSON).

8.5 Right to Object

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data. Since we process data only for licensing purposes, the practical effect of this objection would be termination of your license.

8.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in Section 12. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

9. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions where a legal basis for processing is required, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing your email address and device information is necessary to deliver and manage your license (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interest: Processing device identifiers for license enforcement and fraud prevention (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR). Our legitimate interest is protecting the integrity of our licensing system, balanced against the minimal privacy impact of device identifiers.
  • Consent: When you voluntarily submit your email address for a trial license, you consent to our processing it for that purpose (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

10. International Data Transfers

Our licensing servers may be located in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. By using our licensing services, you acknowledge that your data (limited to the information described in Section 2) may be transferred to and processed in such jurisdictions. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete such information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
